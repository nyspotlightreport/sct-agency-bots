# OFFICIAL ISSN REGISTRATION

## Current Status
- ISSN 2026-0147: Self-declared, NOT in official ISSN registry
- Self-declared ISSNs are common for small publications pre-registration
- Not illegal, but unverifiable by PR firms, journalists, and Google News

## How to Register (Free, 10 minutes to apply, 2-4 weeks to receive)

### US Library of Congress (Recommended)
URL: https://www.loc.gov/issn/

1. Go to loc.gov/issn
2. Click "Request an ISSN"
3. Fill in:
   - Title: NY Spotlight Report
   - Medium: Online
   - URL: https://nyspotlightreport.com
   - Publisher: S.C. Thomas / NY Spotlight Report
   - Coverage: NYC and Long Island Entertainment, Culture, Nightlife, Fashion
   - First issue date: 2020
   - Frequency: Daily
4. Submit - they will assign an official ISSN within 2-4 weeks

## After Registration
1. Replace 2026-0147 with official number sitewide
2. Submit to Google News Publisher Center (requires valid ISSN)
3. List on WorldCat for press/academic credibility
