# Gumroad Product Listing Instructions

## Step-by-Step Guide to List Products on Gumroad

### Prerequisites
- Gumroad account at gumroad.com (use nyspotlightreport@gmail.com)
- Product PDFs from data/kdp_books/ directory
- Cover images from site/downloads/covers/

### For Each Product:

1. **Log in** to gumroad.com
2. Click **"New Product"**
3. **Product Name:** Use exact title from gumroad_products.json
4. **Price:** Set to $4.99-$6.99 (planners: $4.99, guides: $6.99, bundles: $9.99)
5. **Description:** Write 2-3 sentences about what the product includes
6. **Cover Image:** Upload the matching cover from site/downloads/covers/
7. **File:** Upload the PDF from data/kdp_books/
8. **URL slug:** Use lowercase-hyphenated version of title
9. Click **"Publish"**
