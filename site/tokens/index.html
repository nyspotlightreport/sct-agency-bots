Google OAuth — One Click Required
Open Google Console →
Click above → scroll to "Publishing status" → click "Publish App" → confirm. This is Google's verification gate — cannot be done via API. Takes 10 seconds.
Shopify Entity Name — One Click Required
Open Shopify Settings →
Click above → "Business details" section → change "My Store - entity" to "NY Spotlight Report" → Save. Shopify's entity/legal name field is read-only via API.
