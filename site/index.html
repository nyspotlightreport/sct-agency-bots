The Problem

Most entrepreneurs never start it.

You know consistent content builds authority, traffic, and revenue. The problem is execution — it's 20+ hours a week and most people quit by month 2.

No time to create

A quality SEO blog post takes 3–5 hours. You need 4 per week to rank. That's a part-time job you didn't sign up for.
