🟢 Shopify Entity Fix

The payments entity legal name requires Shopify's UI — it's in their billing compliance layer. This opens the exact page and tells you the one field to change.

Open Shopify Settings → Change Entity Name →
Exactly what to do on that page:
1. Scroll to "Business details" section
2. Find "Legal business name" / entity name field
3. Change from "My Store - entity" to NY Spotlight Report
4. Click Save
5. Done — 90 seconds total
