Best Email Marketing Tools for Creators and Businesses 2026

NY Spotlight Report  ·  Updated March 2026

Email Still Converts Better Than Social Media

In 2026, email marketing ROI averages $36 for every $1 spent. The right platform makes all the difference. Here's the honest breakdown.

Best for Newsletter Creators: Beehiiv

Beehiiv dominates for newsletter operators. Their built-in ad network, referral program, and paid subscription management are designed specifically for creator monetization. The 25% affiliate commission and generous free tier make it easy to recommend honestly.

Best for Automation Sequences: ConvertKit

ConvertKit (now rebranded as Kit) wins on automation complexity. Their visual automation builder and commerce features work well for course creators and digital product sellers. The 30% recurring affiliate commission is one of the best in the space.

Best for Businesses: HubSpot Email

HubSpot integrates email with their full CRM. If you're tracking leads and deals alongside email performance, the combined data is unmatched. The free tier includes basic email sending.

Bottom Line

Creators: start with Beehiiv. Businesses: start with HubSpot free. Course creators: ConvertKit. All three have free tiers that let you validate before committing to paid plans.
