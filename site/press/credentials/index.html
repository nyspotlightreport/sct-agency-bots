About the Editor

S.C. Thomas — the credentialed journalist behind every byline at NY Spotlight Report

S.C. Thomas

Founder & Editor-in-Chief, NY Spotlight Report

A multiple-time published author and entertainment journalist with over a decade covering New York City’s cultural landscape, S.C. Thomas founded NY Spotlight Report in 2020 to create an independent editorial voice for the city’s entertainment, fashion, and culture scenes. A U.S. military veteran, Thomas brings precision, authority, and genuine passion for New York storytelling to every issue. His coverage spans Broadway and theater, New York Fashion Week, awards season, NYC nightlife, LGBTQIA+ arts, and the cultural institutions that make New York the entertainment capital of the world.
