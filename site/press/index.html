About the Publication

NY Spotlight Report is an independent digital publication based in New York City, covering the city's entertainment, live performance, fashion, culture, and nightlife scenes. Founded in 2020 by S.C. Thomas — a U.S. Military Veteran and entertainment industry insider — the publication delivers reporting from inside the rooms where decisions are made, performances are born, and cultural movements take shape.

We operate with complete editorial independence. No corporate sponsors influence our coverage. No PR teams approve stories. No advertisers dictate editorial direction. Our commitment is to the reader and to the truth.

Press Credentials & Event Access

NY Spotlight Report holds press credentials for coverage of New York City entertainment, fashion, cultural, and nightlife events. We are actively seeking press accreditation and access for:

  • Broadway and Off-Broadway — opening nights, press previews, backstage access
  • New York Fashion Week — runway shows, backstage, designer interviews
  • Film festivals — Tribeca, New York Film Festival, screenings, premiere coverage
  • Gallery and museum openings — Chelsea, Lower East Side, major institutions
  • Nightlife and venue openings — launch events, DJ residencies, club culture
  • Live performances — concerts, comedy shows, spoken word, dance
  • Industry events — conferences, panels, awards ceremonies
