How to Sell Digital Products Online and Earn Passive Income 2026

NY Spotlight Report  ·  Updated March 2026

Digital Products: The Best Passive Income Model

Create once. Sell forever. Zero shipping, zero inventory, zero marginal cost per sale. Here's the complete playbook for 2026.

What Sells Best

Templates and planners (Notion, Excel, Google Sheets). Prompt packs for AI tools. Design assets (Canva templates, icons, fonts). Educational guides and swipe files. Checklists and SOPs for specific industries. All of these solve specific problems with a specific audience who will pay $7-97 for the solution.

Where to Sell

Gumroad and Etsy handle discovery and delivery automatically. Gumroad charges 10% per sale. Etsy charges $0.20/listing + 6.5% transaction fee. Both have built-in audiences that find your products organically.

Email List as Multiplier

Beehiiv or ConvertKit turn your product buyers into a recurring revenue base. Every product purchase is an email list signup. Every newsletter is a product promotion. The flywheel compounds.

Pricing Psychology

$7-12 impulse tier. $27-47 core offer. $97-197 premium bundle. Price based on value delivered, not hours spent creating. A 2-page checklist that saves someone $500 is worth $27.

Bottom Line

Start with one product. Validate demand at the $7-12 tier. Build an email list around buyers. Create a premium bundle. The entire system can generate $500-2,000/month passively once the flywheel is running.
