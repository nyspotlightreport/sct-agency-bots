Best Online Courses for Building Income Streams in 2026

NY Spotlight Report  ·  Updated March 2026

Courses That Actually Build Income Skills

The online learning space is flooded with courses that teach you to buy more courses. These are the exceptions — skills with direct income applications.

Teachable — Where to Sell Your Own Course

Teachable handles all the infrastructure: video hosting, student management, payment processing, and affiliate program management. Their 30% recurring affiliate commission makes it genuinely worth recommending. The free plan lets you validate demand before paying for hosted infrastructure.

SEO and Content Marketing

Courses on keyword research and SEO using Ahrefs or SEMrush are among the highest-ROI skills to learn. One article ranking #1 for a buyer-intent keyword can generate $500-2,000/month in affiliate commissions indefinitely.

Email Marketing

Learning to build and monetize an email list with ConvertKit or Beehiiv is one of the highest-leverage income skills in 2026. Email marketing consistently returns $36 for every $1 spent.

Bottom Line

Prioritize skills with direct income translation: SEO, email marketing, digital product creation. Avoid courses that are primarily about buying more courses.
