Best Print-on-Demand Sites for Passive Income in 2026

NY Spotlight Report  ·  Updated March 2026

Print-on-Demand: Still One of the Best Passive Income Models

Upload a design once. Earn royalties every time it sells. No inventory, no shipping, no customer service. Here's where to list your designs in 2026.

Redbubble — Best for Discovery

Redbubble's marketplace sends organic traffic to your designs with zero marketing effort on your part. A catalog of 50+ quality designs earns $100-500/month passively. Royalty rate is set by you (typically 15-25% markup above base cost). Best for: artists, quote designs, niche fandom content.

Teepublic — Best Royalty Rate for T-Shirts

Teepublic pays $4 flat per standard tee sold and has a loyal buyer community. Upload the exact same designs as Redbubble for double the distribution with zero extra design work. Best for: bold typographic designs, pop culture.

Amazon Merch on Demand — Highest Traffic

Amazon's print-on-demand program puts your designs in front of Amazon's 200M+ Prime members. Approval is required and can take weeks, but the traffic volume is unmatched. Best for: evergreen designs that work at scale.

Society6 — Best for Premium Art Prints

Society6 customers pay premium prices for art prints, framed prints, and home decor. Higher price points mean higher absolute royalties per sale. Best for: fine art, illustration, photography-style designs.

The Multi-Platform Strategy

Upload every design to all four platforms. The incremental work per platform is minutes. A 20-design catalog on four platforms = 80 listings generating income 24/7. This is genuinely the most time-efficient passive income model available.

Bottom Line

Start with Redbubble and Teepublic (easiest approval). Apply to Amazon Merch immediately (takes time to approve). Add Society6 for premium positioning. All four together maximize your design's earning potential.
