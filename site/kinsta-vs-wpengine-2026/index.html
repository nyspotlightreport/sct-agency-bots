Kinsta vs WP Engine: Premium WordPress Hosting Compared

Both are premium managed WordPress hosts. Both are expensive. Here's what you actually get for the money.

Kinsta — Best Performance and UX

Kinsta runs on Google Cloud with custom Nginx, LXD containers, and edge caching. Their MyKinsta dashboard is the best in the managed WordPress space. Every plan includes daily backups, staging, and CDN. Their 10% recurring affiliate commission is strong for a recurring SaaS product.

WP Engine — Best for Enterprise/Agency

WP Engine has deeper enterprise features: Genesis themes included, multisite management, better compliance tooling, and a larger agency ecosystem. The $200+ per referral affiliate payout reflects their higher average contract value.

Who Should Choose What

Growing business with one site: Kinsta. Agency managing 10+ client sites: WP Engine. Budget-conscious: neither — use Bluehost until you need managed hosting.

Bottom Line

Kinsta for performance-first users. WP Engine for agency/enterprise. Both are genuinely excellent — the choice comes down to your specific requirements and budget.
