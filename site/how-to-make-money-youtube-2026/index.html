YouTube Monetization in 2026 — What Actually Works

YouTube Partner Program remains one of the most accessible ad revenue streams for creators. But the real money is in the ecosystem around it.

Ad Revenue (YPP)

You need 1,000 subscribers + 4,000 watch hours (or 10M Shorts views) to qualify. CPM varies wildly — finance, business, and tech channels earn $10-30 CPM vs $2-5 for entertainment. A 50,000-view channel in the right niche earns $200-800/month from ads alone.

Affiliate Links in Descriptions

This is where YouTube compounds passive income. A 2-year-old video with 50K views still drives affiliate clicks daily. Programs like HubSpot (up to $1,000/sale), Ahrefs ($200/sale), and Beehiiv (25% recurring) turn existing views into ongoing income.

Course Sales via YouTube

YouTube builds trust, then your Teachable course converts that trust into revenue. A 10,000-subscriber channel in a skill-based niche can drive $2,000-10,000/month in course sales with 1-2 video mentions per month.

Shorts Strategy

Shorts monetize differently — lower RPM but much faster growth. Use Shorts to grow subscribers, long-form to monetize them. AI tools like ElevenLabs enable daily Shorts production without on-camera time.

Bottom Line

Start publishing. Consistency matters more than production quality for the first 100 videos. Add affiliate links from day one — they pay even before YPP qualification.
