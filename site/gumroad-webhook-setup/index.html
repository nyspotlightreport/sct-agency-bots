Gumroad Webhook Setup (One-Time, 2 Minutes)

This sets up automatic PDF delivery to every buyer — no manual work ever again.

Step 1 — Go to Gumroad Settings → Integrations

Open: app.gumroad.com/settings/integrations

Step 2 — Add Ping URL (Webhook)

Scroll to "Ping" section. Paste this URL:

https://nyspotlightreport.com/.netlify/functions/gumroad-delivery

Click Save. Done.

Step 3 — Test It

Buy any of your own products for $0 (set price to $0 temporarily, test, set back). You'll receive the download email within 10 seconds.

✅ After this is set: every buyer automatically receives their PDF download link via email. Forever. No more manual uploads needed.
