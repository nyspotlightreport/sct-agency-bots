I Was Not Invited to the Death of Rasputin Curated Showcase. Here Is What That Felt Like.

By S.C. Thomas  |  February 23, 2026  |  NY Spotlight Report

Although I did not have the privilege of attending the curated showcase myself, the whispers from those who did — the descriptions of Petrograd reconstructed inside the bones of the former McKittrick, of familiar characters moving through rooms that once belonged to another world entirely — made the anticipation for what Artemis is Burning is building at 530 West 27th Street feel almost unbearable.
