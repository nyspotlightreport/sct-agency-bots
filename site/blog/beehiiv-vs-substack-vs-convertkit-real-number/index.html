The Real Cost Problem

Content marketing isn't just expensive. It's inconsistently expensive. A writer calls in sick. A social post gets missed. The newsletter goes out late. Each failure compounds — you pay the same amount whether output happens or not.

The alternative isn't hiring better people. It's removing the human dependency entirely for work that doesn't require human judgment.

How the Automated System Works
