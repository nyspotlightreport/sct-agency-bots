Passive Income NY Spotlight Report

25 Zero-Cost Passive Income Streams That Actually Work in 2026

Everything on this list costs $0 to start. Some require an upfront time investment. All can run without ongoing manual work once set up. These aren't theories — they're methods tested and running right now.

Digital Products (Highest ROI)

1. Gumroad digital downloads. Create a PDF, template, checklist, or guide once. List it on Gumroad. Buyers get instant download, you get paid. No inventory, no shipping, no manufacturing. We have 10 products generating sales 24/7.

2. KDP low-content books. Amazon's Kindle Direct Publishing lets you sell physical planners, journals, and puzzle books printed on demand. The buyer orders, Amazon prints and ships, you get royalties. Create the interiors with free tools like Canva or AI generators.

3. Etsy digital downloads. 92 million active buyers specifically looking for digital products. Same Gumroad products listed on Etsy reach an entirely new audience with zero additional creation cost.

4. Payhip store. Another marketplace with built-in discovery. List the same products — one creation, multiple storefronts, multiple income streams.

Content Monetization

5. Beehiiv newsletter. At 1,000 subscribers Beehiiv's ad network activates. Sponsors pay $10–50 CPM (per 1,000 subscribers per send). A 5,000-subscriber newsletter sending weekly earns $2,500–12,500 per month from ads alone.

6. YouTube AdSense. Cross the 1,000-subscriber and 4,000-watch-hour threshold, ads enable. YouTube Shorts now share ad revenue. Shorts take 15 minutes to script and upload via AI tools.

7. Medium Partner Program. Write once, earn per read. Old articles keep generating revenue years after publishing. Republish your blog content for additional income from the same work.

8. NewsBreak Publisher. Pays $1–5 per 1,000 views on news and lifestyle content. Republishing content from other platforms takes 5 minutes per piece.

9. Vocal.media. Pays $3.80 per 1,000 reads. Similar concept to Medium — write once, earn passively forever.

Affiliate Marketing

10. Amazon Associates. 1–10% commission on anything sold through your links. Every blog post mentioning any product becomes a commission opportunity.

11. Beehiiv affiliate program. 50% of the first year's revenue for any paying subscriber you refer. One referral to a $99/month plan = $594 over 12 months.

12. Hosting affiliates (Hostinger, Bluehost). $60–150 per referred customer. One mention in a "how to start a blog" article can generate thousands in commissions over its lifetime.

13. SaaS affiliate programs. ConvertKit, HubSpot, Shopify, and dozens of others pay 20–30% recurring commissions. One referral pays you monthly for as long as they stay.

Bandwidth Sharing (True Passive)

14. Honeygain. Shares your unused internet bandwidth. Earns $20–40/month per device connected. Set it up once, never touch it again.

15. Traffmonetizer. Same concept. $10–25/month per device. Stacks with Honeygain.

16. Pawns.app. Bandwidth sharing + survey completion. $10–20/month passive from bandwidth alone.

17. Repocket. Another bandwidth app. $5–15/month. The more devices and locations, the higher the earnings.

Print-on-Demand

18. Redbubble. Upload designs, they handle printing, shipping, and customer service. 15–30% royalty per sale. One design can sell for years.

19. TeePublic. Same model, different marketplace. More buyers means more sales from the same designs.

20. Merch by Amazon. Amazon's POD program. Invite-only but worth applying. Prime buyers, massive reach.

Donations and Tips

21. Ko-fi. Add a "buy me a coffee" button to your site and social profiles. Regular readers and fans support creators voluntarily. Some creators earn $500–2,000/month from tips alone.

22. Buy Me A Coffee. Similar to Ko-fi with the addition of memberships ($5–25/month per patron).

23. Brave Rewards. Verify your website or social profiles with Brave Browser. 50+ million Brave users can tip creators in BAT cryptocurrency with one click.

Compound Systems

24. SEO content + affiliate links. A single blog post ranking on page 1 for a buying-intent keyword can generate $200–2,000/month in affiliate commissions forever. Build 50 of those over two years and you have a $10,000–100,000/month passive income machine.

25. Email list monetization. Every subscriber is worth $1–5/month in direct monetization (product sales, affiliate promotions, sponsored sends). 10,000 subscribers = $10,000–50,000/month potential.

The Compound Effect

None of these make you rich individually. Three bandwidth apps make $50/month. A newsletter ad makes $500/month. Affiliate links make $300/month. A digital product store makes $200/month. Add them up across 10 streams and you're at $1,500–3,000/month passive — all from systems that run without you.

The key is stacking, not replacing. Every stream you add compounds the ones that came before it.
