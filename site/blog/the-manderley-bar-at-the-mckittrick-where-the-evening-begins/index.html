The Manderley Bar at the McKittrick: Where the Evening Begins

By S.C. Thomas  |  September 22, 2024  |  NY Spotlight Report

As the heart of New York's immersive theater scene beats steadily, the McKittrick Hotel continues to be the epicenter of intrigue and atmosphere. This enigmatic venue, known for its flagship production "Sleep No More," is not just a haven for the theatrical arts but also houses its own cabalistic watering hole: The Manderley Bar. An evening spent here is less about quenching thirst and more about setting the stage for a night steeped in mystery and allure.

The Manderley Bar, tucked away within the McKittrick's labyrinthine halls, serves as the prelude to the night's main event. Entering the space feels akin to stepping through a portal into a bygone era, where the boundary between fantasy and reality blurs. Art deco touches meld with dimly lit corners, and the air is thick with the nostalgia of a 1930s speakeasy, coaxing patrons to leave the mundane at the door.

It's a space that revels in the details—crimson velvet drapes, flickering candlelight, and a stage that beckons the musically inclined to spin tales of their own. The bar itself is a marvel, offering craft cocktails that are as much an art form as the performance about to unfold. Mixologists here serve up liquid alchemy, with concoctions that nod to the theatrical, each sip designed to transport the drinker further into the McKittrick's world. Favorites include the “Punchdrunk”, a concoction that playfully tips its hat to the innovative creators behind “Sleep No More,” offering a complex blend of flavors that mirror the narrative layers the audience will soon peel away.

At the Manderley, the atmosphere is electric and brimming with promise. Conversations are murmured, secrets are exchanged, and the anticipation is palpable. The bar serves as the perfect antechamber to the immersive odyssey that is "Sleep No More." Here, guests are free to speculate on the tale of Macbeth reimagined, to plot their course through the evening's performance, or simply to absorb the ambiance that has made the McKittrick a sanctuary for the unconventional.

The magic of the Manderley Bar lies not just in its aesthetic but in its role as a facilitator of connection. Whether it's the shared experience of a haunting jazz tune or a fleeting glance exchanged with a stranger across the room, the bar creates a sense of community, albeit temporary, among its patrons. It is a place where the mundane is eclipsed by the extraordinary and where time itself seems to pause, holding each moment in a suspended state of wonder.

As the clock ticks closer to the commencement of "Sleep No More," the air grows thicker with anticipation. The transition from the Manderley Bar to the performance itself is seamless, as guests are gently coaxed from the familiar embrace of the bar into the unknown corridors of the McKittrick. The experience is designed to disorient and thrill, ensuring that the story begins even before the performance proper unfolds.

In a city teeming with nightlife options, the Manderley Bar stands apart, not just as a place to enjoy a pre-show drink but as an integral part of the narrative journey curated by the McKittrick Hotel. It is a testament to the power of immersive theater and the unique role a well-crafted atmosphere can play in storytelling. Here, the evening doesn't just begin—it is conjured into being, promising an unforgettable journey that transcends the confines of traditional theater.

The Manderley Bar remains an essential preamble for anyone seeking to lose themselves in the world of "Sleep No More." It is where the night’s enchantment is first tasted and where every sip, every whisper, every note is a harbinger of the magic that awaits. As the McKittrick continues to captivate New York with its unique blend of theater and mystery, the Manderley Bar ensures that each visit is more than mere entertainment—it is an experience, a dreamscape where the evening’s reverie takes its first breath.
