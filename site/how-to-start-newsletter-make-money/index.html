How to Start a Newsletter and Make Money in 2026

NY Spotlight Report  ·  Updated March 2026

Newsletters Are Still the Best Passive Income Vehicle

While social media algorithms throttle reach and platforms come and go, your email list is an asset you own. Here's how to build one that earns.

Choose the Right Platform

Beehiiv is the top choice in 2026 for monetization. Their built-in ad network pays you per subscriber — meaning you earn from day one without selling anything. The 25% affiliate commission also makes it a top referral program.

ConvertKit is better for automation-heavy sequences. Their 30% recurring commission is one of the best in email marketing.

Monetization Methods

Sponsorships: Once you hit 1,000 subscribers, you can charge $50-200 per newsletter issue from sponsors. At 5,000 subscribers, rates jump to $500-2,000/issue.

Paid tiers: Offer exclusive content for $5-10/month. Even 100 paid subscribers = $500-1,000/month.

Affiliate links: Naturally recommend tools you use. High-commission programs like Ahrefs ($200/sale) and SEMrush ($200/sale) convert well with warm audiences.

Digital products: Your newsletter list is your best customer base for selling your own products.

Growth Tactics That Work

Beehiiv's referral program lets readers refer friends for rewards — this compounds growth without paid ads. SEO articles (like this one) also drive consistent organic signups when properly optimized.

Bottom Line

Start free on Beehiiv. Write one good issue per week. Focus on a specific niche. Monetize with Beehiiv's ad network immediately. Add paid tier at 500 subscribers. The compounding effect of a growing list makes newsletters one of the most reliable passive income vehicles available.
