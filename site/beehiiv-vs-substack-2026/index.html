Beehiiv vs Substack — Which Newsletter Platform Pays More in 2026

NY Spotlight Report  ·  Updated March 2026

Beehiiv vs Substack: The Real Comparison

Both platforms are serious contenders for newsletter creators in 2026. The right choice depends entirely on your monetization strategy.

Beehiiv — Best for Ad Revenue and Growth

Beehiiv's built-in ad network monetizes your list automatically from day one. Their referral program compounds growth without paid ads. For creators who want platform-powered monetization without direct selling, Beehiiv wins. The 25% affiliate commission also reflects genuine confidence in their product.

Substack — Best for Paid Subscriptions

Substack's network effect drives discovery better than Beehiiv for pure subscription models. If your content is premium and paid subscribers are the primary goal, Substack's marketplace gives you built-in distribution. They take 10% of revenue vs Beehiiv's 0% on the free tier.

The Hybrid Approach

Many creators run both: Beehiiv as their primary (for automation, ad revenue, and custom domain) and mirror top content to Substack for discovery. ConvertKit is the third option for creators who want maximum automation over built-in network effects.

Bottom Line

New creators monetizing through ads + affiliate: Beehiiv. Premium subscription-first: Substack. Heavy automation needed: ConvertKit.
